# Songvault

A home for your songs. Store, organize, and revisit your ideas whenever inspiration hits. Each entry keeps your creative process organized.

## How It Works
* Click “Create Songs” to open the form
* Enter your song title and lyrics as these are required fields
* You can add a link to an instrumental and choose a genre
* Click “Save Song”
* “View Songs” shows your entries
* You can click into cards to expand them

## Features
* You can save multiple songs locally
* You can delete and edit entries
* The entries behave like cards that expand
